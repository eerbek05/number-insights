Categorical data — χ² tests

One row of counts → goodness-of-fit against a uniform distribution. Two or more rows → independence test on the contingency table.

Two-sample comparison — Welch's t-test

Welch's test does not assume equal variances. Cohen's d: ≈0.2 small · ≈0.5 medium · ≈0.8 large.

Paired analysis — paired t-test & correlation

One-way ANOVA

Tests whether all group means are equal. A small p-value says at least one group differs.
